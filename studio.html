The production pipeline

Script to Screen in 4 Stages

NerdCommand Studio orchestrates every stage of production — no individual tool-wrangling, no manual assembly. One command kicks off the whole chain.

01
📝
Script & Shot Bible
Write your script or prompt the AI to generate one. Studio parses it into a structured shot bible — scene by scene, character by character.
02
🎨
Image Generation
Every scene is rendered as a still or video clip via ComfyUI, Wan2.2, or CogView — with your character and style locked in via consistency prompts.
03
🎞️
Cinematic Assembly
Segments are encoded with grade presets (CINEMATIC, NOIR, FLASHBACK), title cards, audio fades, and letterbox — then losslessly concatenated.
04
🚀
Export & Distribute
Final cut exports at 1280×720. Social cuts — Reels, TikTok, LinkedIn — are automatically trimmed and formatted from the same assembly.

Studio features

Everything a Filmmaker Needs.
Nothing They Don't.

🎬
Full Cinematic Grade System
Five professional color presets built in — one flag in your command and the entire output gets graded. No color work required. CINEMATIC gives you film-look warm shadows and punchy highlights. NOIR drops saturation and cranks contrast. FLASHBACK adds a sepia warmth with slightly crushed black. COOL and WARM handle the rest.
CINEMATIC NOIR FLASHBACK WARM COOL
🧬
Character Consistency Engine
Your characters look the same in every shot, every scene, every universe. Consistency prompts are locked to each character profile and injected automatically into every generation.
⚡
Auto-Recover on Error
If any segment fails during render, Studio diagnoses, fixes, and re-runs that segment only. The rest of your film keeps building. Full productions never abort on a single error.
📋
Timeline JSON + Edit Plan
Every production generates a structured timeline.json, edit_plan.md, and scene_index.csv — so your work is documented, version-controlled, and resumable at any point.
📱
Auto Social Cuts
Reels, TikToks, and LinkedIn posts are cut from your feature automatically. Caption copy and hashtags generated from the scene data. Post schedule output to your social queue.
